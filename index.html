History



Vincent Porcaro Inc. (VPI) is a performance-driven third party logistics company that was started 17 years ago in 1996. What started out as a local business in New England grew steadily and expanded into a nationwide company. Now, VPI is a globally recognized company extending as far as Ontario, California & Shenzhen, China. With a corporate office in Providence, Rhode Island on Historic Federal Hill Business District, VPI has offered a variety of services to our customers throughout the years and continues to offer more as technology rapidly grows. These services include assembly, fulfillment and distribution of literature, displays, Point-Of-Purchase (POP) and pick and pack, warehousing and logistics. POP and literature fulfillment has been a specialty at VPI for many years supported by state of the art technology enabling us to provide extraordinary results in this very important fulfillment process.

Support

VPI supports companies such as Wal-Mart, Yankee Candle, Foster Grant International, A.T. Cross Co., and Jones Apparel, with monthly rollouts; display and kit assemblies, and daily pick and pack orders.

Our commitment to our customers remains to develop the most cost efficient logistical merchandise distribution network. As VPI continues to grow, we are striving to keep our customers goal and the success of their business as our main priority.

VPI wants to serve as a trusted partner to all of our employees. We want to add value to your business and make all supply chain processes convenient and efficient to ensure future success to all who are involved. We want to support the growing goals and demands of the customer and be able to accommodate any supply chain sizes that they have to offer. The customer’s goals and results is our main focus. We want to work to improve deliveries, reduce costs to the customers and lower risk and reduction of inventory levels.

Vision

As a leader in the third party logistics industry we also want to constantly be improving and upgrading our processes to better serve our customers. By delivering new ideas frequently we can instill in the customer that we are the right choice for all their logistics needs. We want the work we do and the work that customers give us to be a strategic partnership, working together to better both parties involved. All of the services processes and technology that we provide will be at use to achieve the specific goals that the customer has.
