Walmart is an American multinational retail corporation that operates a chain of hypermarkets, discount department stores, and grocery stores. Talbots is an American speciality retailer and direct marketer of women's clothing, shoes and fashion accessories. Talbots also started locally in Hingham, Massachusets and has since grown nation wide as well as an expanding e-commerce business. Talbots shoppers take delight in shopping for a mixture of heritage and innovation.

Staples, Inc. is an American multinational office supply retailing corporation. It currently comprises over 1,500 stores in North America. Headquartered in Framingham, Massachusets, Staples also does business extensively with enterprises in the United States and Canada. Staples became a member of the Fortune 500 on its tenth anniversary in 1996. Staples has also grown outside the country, having stores in Canada, Argentina, Australia, etc.
